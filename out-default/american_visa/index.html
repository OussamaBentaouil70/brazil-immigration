FAQ - Immigration Consulting (English Translation)

Clear answers about visas, Green Cards, family options, and the immigration process in the United States.

What are the advantages of hiring an immigration consulting firm?

Hiring an immigration consulting firm offers several advantages, including specialized guidance throughout the entire immigration process, in-depth knowledge of laws and regulations, personalized support, time and effort savings in preparing documents and forms, increased chances of success in obtaining the desired visa, and continuous follow-up throughout the entire process.

What is the process for obtaining a Green Card in the USA?

The process for obtaining a Green Card in the USA involves several steps, including determining eligibility, applying for the appropriate visa, completing forms, gathering supporting documents, and attending interviews. Our team of immigration experts is ready to guide you through each step of this process.

What are the requirements to qualify for a work visa in the USA?

The requirements to qualify for a work visa in the USA may vary depending on the type of visa. Generally, it is necessary to have a job offer from a U.S. employer, specific qualifications for the position, proof of relevant professional experience, and demonstrate that the employer was unable to find a qualified candidate in the USA for the position.

How long does it take to obtain an immigrant visa?

Processing time for an immigrant visa can vary depending on the type of visa, application volume, and other factors. On average, it can take several months to over a year to obtain an immigrant visa. Our team is committed to expediting the process and providing regular updates on the status of your application.

Can I bring my family to the USA with me?

Yes, it is possible to bring your family to the USA, provided they meet the specific requirements and processes for each family member. Children under 21 years of age and spouses may be eligible to accompany you in the immigration process. Our team can guide you on the available options for bringing your family with you.

What are the options available for studying in the USA?

There are several options for studying in the USA, such as the F-1 student visa for academic courses, the M-1 student visa for vocational courses, and the J-1 exchange program. Each option has specific requirements and permitted duration. We can help you identify the option best suited to your profile and educational goals.

Is it possible to start a business in the USA as a foreigner?

Yes, it is possible to start a business in the USA as a foreigner. There are different types of entrepreneur visas, such as the E-2 visa for investors, the L-1 visa for transferring employees from a foreign company to a U.S. subsidiary, and the EB-5 visa for investors in projects that create jobs. Our team can guide you on the available options and requirements for each type of visa.

How can I check the status of my visa application?

You can check the status of your visa application through the U.S. Department of State's online system. Our team will also provide regular updates on the progress of your process and will be available to answer any questions related to the status of your application.

What should I do if my visa application is denied?

If your visa application is denied, our team will analyze the reasons for the denial and evaluate the available options to appeal the decision or explore other immigration pathways. We will do our best to seek alternative solutions and work together to achieve the desired outcome.

How can immigration consulting help me after I obtain my visa?

Our immigration consulting firm offers comprehensive support even after you obtain your visa. We can assist you in adapting to the new country, guide you on issues related to work, education, health, and other needs, as well as provide guidance on the process of obtaining permanent residency or citizenship, if applicable.

Is it possible to apply for an immigrant visa while I am in the USA?

Yes, it is possible to apply for an immigrant visa while you are in the USA, provided you meet the specific requirements and are within a visa category eligible for change of status within the country. Our team can analyze your situation and help you determine the best option for your case.

What is the difference between a non-immigrant visa and an immigrant visa?

A non-immigrant visa is intended for people who wish to enter the USA temporarily, whether for tourism, study, temporary work, or other specific purposes. An immigrant visa, on the other hand, is for those who wish to move permanently to the USA and obtain permanent residency (Green Card).

How much does it cost to hire an immigration consulting firm?

The costs associated with hiring an immigration consulting firm may vary depending on the services requested and the complexity of the case. Our team offers an initial consultation to discuss your needs and provide an estimate of the fees involved. We will make every effort to offer accessible and transparent services.

Can I complete the immigration process on my own, without the help of a consulting firm?

While it is possible to complete the immigration process on your own, it is important to keep in mind that the U.S. immigration system can be complex and subject to frequent changes. Hiring an experienced immigration consulting firm can offer significant advantages, such as specialized guidance, reduced errors, time savings, and increased chances of success. Our team is prepared to help you at every stage of the process and provide reliable support.

What is the average processing time for an immigrant visa?

The average processing time for an immigrant visa varies depending on the type of visa and the individual processing of the case. In general, processing times can range from a few months to over a year. It is important to be aware that processing times can be influenced by various factors, such as demand, case complexity, and potential bureaucratic delays.

What are the financial requirements to obtain an immigrant visa?

The financial requirements to obtain an immigrant visa also depend on the type of visa you are applying for. Some visas may require proof of sufficient financial resources to support yourself in the USA, while others may require significant investments. It is important to consult the specific guidelines for the desired visa or contact us for detailed information about financial requirements.

Can I work in the USA while waiting for my immigrant visa approval?

This depends on the type of visa you hold and the work permissions associated with it. Some immigrant visas allow you to work in the USA during the immigration process, while others may have restrictions or require a separate work authorization. It is important to understand the conditions of your specific visa and comply with U.S. immigration laws.

What are the benefits of obtaining a Green Card compared to a temporary visa?

Obtaining a Green Card, that is, permanent residency in the USA, brings several benefits compared to a temporary visa. With a Green Card, you have the right to live and work permanently in the USA, as well as access to social benefits, education, healthcare, and other rights guaranteed to permanent residents. Additionally, a Green Card offers greater stability and security, as it is not subject to expiration dates or renewal restrictions like temporary visas. You will also have the opportunity to apply for U.S. citizenship after meeting the requirements.

What are the options available for international students who wish to study in the USA?

There are several options available for international students who wish to study in the USA. The F-1 student visa is one of the most common options and allows you to study at accredited academic institutions in the USA. Additionally, there are specific visas for exchange programs, language courses, and vocational training. Our immigration consulting team can help you understand the different options and guide you on the requirements and processes involved.

How can I know if I am eligible for a specific immigrant visa?

Eligibility for an immigrant visa depends on several factors, including your immigration purpose, qualifications, experience, family ties, and other visa-specific criteria. It is highly recommended to seek guidance from an experienced immigration consultant, who will analyze your case individually, assess your background and personal circumstances, and provide an accurate eligibility assessment for the desired visa.

Is it possible to obtain an immigrant visa to work as a self-employed professional in the USA?

Yes, it is possible to obtain an immigrant visa to work as a self-employed professional in the USA. The most common visa for self-employed workers is the employment-based visa (H-1B for skilled professionals, O-1 for individuals with extraordinary abilities, etc.). However, specific requirements and criteria vary for each type of visa, and it is important to understand the available options and meet the established requirements.

What are the tax implications of obtaining a Green Card in the USA?

Obtaining a Green Card in the USA can have significant tax implications. As a permanent resident, you are generally considered a U.S. tax resident and are subject to tax obligations, including filing income tax returns and paying taxes on worldwide income. It is important to consult an accounting professional or tax specialist to ensure proper compliance with U.S. tax laws and understand your specific tax responsibilities.

Can I apply for an immigrant visa while I am in the USA as a tourist?

In general, it is not recommended to apply for an immigrant visa while in the USA as a tourist. Most immigrant visas require you to be outside the USA at the time of application and attend consular interviews in your country of origin. However, there are some exceptions and specific programs that may allow for change of status while you are in the USA. It is essential to consult an experienced immigration consultant to assess your situation and determine the best approach for applying for an immigrant visa.

What are the options available for entrepreneurs who wish to start a business in the USA?

There are several options available for entrepreneurs who wish to start a business in the USA. The E-2 investor visa is a popular option for foreign investors who wish to start or acquire a business in the USA. Additionally, the EB-5 visa offers the opportunity to obtain a Green Card through a substantial investment in a U.S. company. It is important to understand the specific requirements and obtain professional guidance to understand the available options and meet the required criteria.

How long does it take to obtain an EB2-NIW visa?

Processing time for the EB2-NIW visa can vary, but it generally takes from 6 months to 1 year. This period includes petition preparation, submission to USCIS (U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services), review, and possible requests for additional evidence. It is important to keep in mind that processing times can be affected by demand and administrative procedures.

Can I apply for the EB2-NIW visa on my own, or should I seek assistance from an immigration attorney?

While it is not mandatory to hire an immigration attorney to apply for the EB2-NIW visa, it is highly recommended. The application process is complex and requires detailed and specific documentation. An experienced immigration attorney can guide and represent the applicant throughout the entire process, increasing the chances of success and avoiding errors that could delay or harm the petition. It is important to choose a qualified and trustworthy professional to assist in obtaining the EB2-NIW visa.

If you have more questions about the immigration process, our immigration consulting team is ready to help. Contact us by phone, email, or schedule a consultation to discuss your specific needs. We are committed to providing clear answers, reliable guidance, and personalized solutions for your questions and concerns.
