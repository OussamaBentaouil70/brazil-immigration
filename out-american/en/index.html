I entered Brazil as a visitor. Can I extend my stay with the Federal Police?

It depends.

For some nationalities, based on reciprocity, an extension is allowed. Documents may include proof of subsistence, invitation letter, airline ticket, application form, and service fee payment.

What is the time frame to use the visa?

The visa validity period is the period during which the visa may be used for entry into Brazil.

Visa validity is different from residence authorization period after registration with the Federal Police.

  • Visitor Visa: usually 1 year, multiple entries while valid
  • Temporary Visa: up to 1 year, multiple entries while valid
My address has changed. What should I do?

Foreign residents must keep information updated with the Federal Police Migration Department.

My passport is expiring. Can I still have the Brazilian visa issued in it?

To issue a visa, the passport should have at least 6 months validity and blank pages.

Who can I bring to Brazil through family reunification?

Family reunification may include spouse/partner, children and other relatives under legal conditions.
